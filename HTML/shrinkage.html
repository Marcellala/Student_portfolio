Overview

Problem

The average bar sees a 15-25% loss profit due to shrinkage. There are several factors attributable to this loss including damage/spills, over pouring, waste, theft, or simply administrative errors. Owners and managers don’t have a simple, efficient, and affordable way to manage their inventory.

My Role

Product Designer, UX Researcher

Solution

Shrinkage provides bars and nightclubs with a simple way to manage inventory, place and receive orders, and track shrinkage. Our application encourages users to track inventory to help them better understand key aspects of their beverage program and avoid financial losses.

Customize Bar

Quickly add Vendors and Products during setup

Setup begins by adding vendors to the user’s custom ‘virtual’ bar, then syncing contact information for easy communication with vendors and sales representatives.

Next, Swipe & Set technology allows users to quickly create a master product list by adding products to their two main stock areas - liquor closet and cooler. Users can search our database, scan UPC codes, or add custom products.

To complete setup, the user customizes their bar with unique areas and quickly adds products from the master list by swiping them into each custom area until the virtual bar is complete.

Taking Inventory

Complete full inventory with ease

To conduct inventory, users move through each area of their bar and take inventory of each product. The application accounts for open and closed products.

Multiple users can take inventory at the same time to help decrease labor costs. One user submits the final inventory to generate orders.

Submitting orders

Send orders from anywhere

Once inventory has been completed, the application generates orders for users based on PAR values and inventory on hand. Users can view, edit, and submit orders to their Sales Rep by email, text, or phone.

When an order is delivered, an invoice is generated from the order that was placed. The user than verifies quantities and items with the physical delivery and can adjust as needed. To account for free and new items, users can add additional line items to the invoice and designate an area within the bar to add the item to inventory.

Once the orders have been placed and received, the inventory is updated with the new quantities.

Bar Management

Manage your bar on the go

Our solution allows bars owners and managers to update their virtual bar anytime from any location. Closing the patio bar for the season? Just mark that area as inactive for now to skip it during inventory. The data is preserved until the area is marked as active again. Update vendor information, remove discontinued product and update pricing all within the app.

Enter cost of goods sold (COGS) and weekly sales for a quick pour cost snapshot. Additional analytics are available on the companion web application dashboard.
